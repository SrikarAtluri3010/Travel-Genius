Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel a booking?

Navigate to 'My Trips', select the booking you want to cancel, and click the 'Cancel' button. Make sure to check the cancellation policy for refunds.

How can I modify my travel dates?

In 'My Trips', choose 'Modify' on the booking you wish to change. Follow the steps to select a new date based on availability.

What if I don't receive my booking confirmation?

Check your spam or junk folder. If not found, contact support via chat or email, and we will resend the confirmation.
